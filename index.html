About Us

SIPS at Virginia Tech brings together students from all across campus who are interested in intellectual property and the surrounding issues. We work with VT faculty, local professionals, and Virginia Tech Intellectual Properties (VTIP) to provide lectures and other events with the goal of increasing awareness of intellectual property on campus.
