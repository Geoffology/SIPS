Synopsis

The Student Intellectual Property Society of Virginia Tech is a group of motivated undergraduate students that work together to further their interests involving the intellectual property field. By working with a multitude of experienced professional, VT faculty, and the Virginia Tech Intellectual Properties, we have increased awareness of intellectual property on campus. Together we hope to further the community, especially Virginia Techs, awareness of IP and further our own through networking and collaborating.
